    What is PETE (Polyethylene Terephthalate?
    PETE is a clear, strong, and lightweight plastic that is widely used for packaging foods & beverages.

    Common goods made with PETE material:
    • Water bottles
    • Soft drink bottles
    • Juice containers
    • Food packaging
    • Cooking oil
    What can it be recycled to?
    • Bottles and polyster fibers
    • Tote bags
    • Fleece garments
    • Life jackets
    • Carpets
    • Pillow stuffing

    Health & Environmental Risks:
    PETE should not be reused. It is intended for single use application. Repeated use will increase risk of bacterial growth.

    Difficulty level to recycle:
  • Easy

  • Curbside recycling.
